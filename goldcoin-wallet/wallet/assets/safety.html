Some words about safety of your GoldCoins:

  • GoldCoins in GoldCoin Wallet are stored on the device. If you lose your device, you'll lose your GoldCoins. Just like money in your physical wallet.
  • Before uninstalling GoldCoin Wallet (or clearing app data/wiping your device), transfer your GoldCoins to another wallet. Any remaining GoldCoins will be lost.
  • Transactions cannot be undone. If you send your GoldCoins into the void, there is no way to get them back unless you know the receiver and he/she is willing to send them back.
  • Keep your mobile device safe! Do only install apps you fully trust. Malicious apps could be trying to steal your wallet.
  • Keep the risk low! Only use GoldCoin Wallet with small amounts for day use, and keep your GoldCoin savings at a safer place.
